The problem

Your health lives in a dozen different apps.

Sleep in one app, heart rate in another, steps and meals somewhere else — each behind its own login, subscription, and cloud you can't see into. There's no single, calm place that just tells you how you're doing today.

The calm alternative

One warm view that brings it all home.

Vitals gathers every source into a single dashboard on your own hardware, distills it into a friendly readiness read, and surfaces the trends that matter — quietly, privately, and entirely yours.

What you'll see

A dashboard that feels like a deep breath.

A friendly readiness ring, sleep, energy in and out, steps, gentle trends, and your daily brief — everything in one calm, glanceable place.

Light & dark

Beautiful, whichever way you like it.

Soft and airy by day, calm and premium by night. Vitals follows your taste with a gentle, flicker-free theme switch.

In your pocket

Install it like any app.

Vitals is a progressive web app — add it to your home screen and it opens full-screen, works offline-first, and feels right at home next to your other health apps.

Your daily brief

A friendly morning read, written by your AI.

Each morning Vitals looks at your overnight data and writes a warm, plain-language brief — readiness, sleep, food, training, and a few gentle suggestions. It's bring-your-own-model: run it fully local with Ollama or LocalAI for 100% privacy and no cloud, or point it at Claude or Codex if you prefer. The local path needs no API key.

  • 100% local Run entirely on your machine with Ollama or LocalAI. Nothing ever leaves the box.
  • Your choice Prefer a cloud model? Point it at Claude or Codex instead. Swap any time.
  • Ask anywhere A built-in MCP server lets you query your data from claude.ai or Claude Desktop.

Yours, by design

Private. Self-hosted. Open.

Vitals runs on your own always-on machine. Every reading lives in a local database on hardware you control.

Truly private

Your data lives in a local database on your own box — a Mac mini or any always-on machine. No vendor cloud, no telemetry. Reach it from anywhere over a private Tailscale network.

Self-hosted

You run Vitals yourself, on your own hardware, on your own terms. Keep it alive with launchd on macOS or systemd on Linux, and it just quietly does its job.

Open source

A readable, friendly codebase you can audit, fork, and extend. Add a wearable, reshape the brief, or run it untouched forever — it answers only to you.

Self-host in minutes

Up and running, gently.

    Clone & install. Grab the repo and install dependencies.
  2. 2
